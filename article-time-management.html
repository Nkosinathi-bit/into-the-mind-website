Time Management Strategies to Reduce Academic Stress

Effective time management can significantly reduce academic stress. These strategies help you stay on top of coursework while maintaining balance in your personal life.

The Eisenhower Matrix

Prioritize tasks by urgency and importance:

Urgent & Important

Do now (e.g., due tomorrow)

Not Urgent & Important

Schedule (e.g., long-term projects)

Urgent & Not Important

Delegate if possible

Not Urgent & Not Important

Eliminate or minimize

The 2-Minute Rule

  1. If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it immediately
  2. Quick wins reduce mental clutter
  3. Examples: replying to short emails, organizing notes

Time Blocking Template

Time Monday Wednesday Friday
9-11 AM Focused study Class + Review Research work
2-4 PM Group project Exercise break Focused study
7-9 PM Light review Social time Self-care

Key Takeaways

  • Plan weekly and daily, but stay flexible
  • Include breaks and self-care in your schedule
  • Review and adjust your system regularly
