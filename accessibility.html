Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making mental health resources accessible to all South Africans

Our Accessibility Commitment

At Into the Mind, we believe mental health resources should be accessible to everyone in South Africa, regardless of ability. We strive to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards and comply with South Africa's Promotion of Access to Information Act.

Implemented Accessibility Features

👁️

Visual Accessibility

  • High contrast color scheme
  • Text resizing without loss of functionality
  • Alternative text for all images
  • Compatibility with screen readers like JAWS and NVDA
👂

Auditory Accessibility

  • Closed captions for all video content
  • Transcripts available in English, Afrikaans, and isiZulu
  • No audio-only content without text alternatives
⌨️

Keyboard Navigation

  • Full keyboard operability
  • Visible focus indicators
  • Skip to content links
  • Logical tab order
🧠

Cognitive Accessibility

  • Plain language content
  • Content available in multiple South African languages
  • Consistent navigation structure
  • No time limits on content

Technical Compliance

Standards Compliance

This website conforms to:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA
  • South African National Standard SANS 556:2021
  • Promotion of Access to Information Act (PAIA)

Accessibility Testing

  • Tested with South African assistive technologies
  • Reviewed by disability organizations
  • Regular audits by SA Web Accessibility Initiative
