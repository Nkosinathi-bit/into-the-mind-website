Self-Care Strategies

Essential practices to maintain your mental health and wellbeing as a student

Introduction

Self-care is not selfish—it's a necessary part of maintaining mental health during your academic journey. These evidence-based strategies can help you build resilience and prevent burnout.

Self-Care Techniques

Essential Physical Care Practices

  • Sleep Hygiene: Maintain consistent sleep schedule (7-9 hours)
  • Nutrition: Balanced meals with regular eating times
  • Movement: 30 minutes of daily activity (walking, yoga, etc.)
  • Hydration: Drink water throughout the day

Quick Tip: Set reminders to stand and stretch every hour during study sessions.

Nurturing Your Emotional Health

  • Journaling: Daily reflection on feelings and experiences
  • Therapy: Regular sessions with a counselor
  • Social Connection: Quality time with supportive people
  • Creative Expression: Art, music, writing as emotional outlets

Quick Tip: Practice naming your emotions without judgment ("I notice I'm feeling anxious").

Balancing Study and Wellbeing

  • Time Management: Realistic scheduling with breaks
  • Study Environment: Organized, comfortable workspace
  • Boundaries: Designated study-free times
  • Growth Mindset: Focus on progress over perfection

Quick Tip: Use the Pomodoro technique (25 min study, 5 min break).
