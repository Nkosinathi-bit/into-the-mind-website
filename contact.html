Frequently Asked Questions

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during weekdays. Messages received on weekends may take until the next business day for a response.

While we can provide general information and resources, we are not a substitute for professional mental health care. We recommend contacting licensed professionals or your university counseling center for personal advice.

We treat all communications with strict confidentiality. However, if we believe there is immediate risk of harm to yourself or others, we may need to contact emergency services. For more details, please review our privacy policy.
