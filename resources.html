South African University Services

University Counseling

Most South African universities offer free counseling services to registered students.

Contact your student wellness center for appointments.

Student Health Services

Many universities include mental health coverage in student health plans.

Check with your campus health center.

Peer Support Groups

Most campuses have student-run mental health initiatives.

Look for notices in student centers or online portals.

Disability Support

If mental health affects your studies, you may qualify for accommodations.

Contact your university's disability unit.
