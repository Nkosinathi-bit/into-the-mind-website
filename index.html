About This Project

"Into the Mind" is an initiative to provide accessible mental health resources for university students. We believe that mental health is just as important as physical health, and we aim to break the stigma surrounding mental health discussions in academic environments.

Our content is carefully curated to address common challenges faced by students, including stress, anxiety, depression, and maintaining healthy relationships.
